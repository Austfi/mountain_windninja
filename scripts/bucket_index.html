Keystone WindNinja

Automated Wind Forecasts

WindNinja is a high-resolution wind modeling tool developed by the USDA Forest Service for fire behavior prediction. It uses terrain data and weather forecasts to compute localized wind fields.

Data source: NOAA HRRR (High-Resolution Rapid Refresh) | Model resolution: 30m | Output height: 10m AGL
